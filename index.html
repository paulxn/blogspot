Tourist Attraction and Culinary of Prabumulih

Prabumulih is a city in South Sumatra that was inaugurated as a city on October 17, 2001. Many people must learn that Prabumulih has tourist attractions, especially souvenir centers. Two interesting tourist attractions in Prabumulih are the Prabumulih city park and the Prabumulih pineapple agrotourism. For culinary purposes, I will visit several Prabumulih souvenir centers to find out what typical Prabumulih foods are produced there. For accessibility, visitors can use private transportation, such as bicycles, motorbikes or cars, to get to these places.

Taman Kota Prabumulih



The first is Prabumulih City Center. Prabumulih City Center is a tourist attraction, a culinary destination, and a place to relax and do sports activities. Prabumulih City Center is located on Ahmad Yani Street, Gunung Ibul, East Prabumulih District, Prabumulih City, South Sumatra.


In the past, this park was a football field, and the field was often used as a night fair tourist attraction. Then, this park was remade or renovated. After completion, this park was officially designated as the first city park on January 15th, 2015.



There is no entrance ticket to Prabumulih City Center. You only have to pay for parking. So, everyone who wants to visit there only needs to pay for parking. Inside Prabumulih City Center are tribunes, culinary outlets, a multi-purpose building and a basketball court. While enjoying the atmosphere of the park, visitors can enjoy the culinary delights in Prabumulih City Center, and too many teenagers play skateboarding.

On weekends, the park will be busy with visitors doing activities such as sports and relaxing. Along the road until entering Prabumulih City Center, it is usually used for culinary events and night markets. So, visitors can enjoy food and play with the rides at the event.

Prabumulih Pineapple Agrotourism



The second is Prabumulih Pineapple Agrotourism. This place is used as a center for developing pineapple cultivation and tourism in Prabumulih City, which began to be planned and built in 2021 and was inaugurated in February 2023.



At Prabumulih Pineapple Agrotourism, visitors can enjoy the vast expanse of pineapple gardens and take photos of the beautified pineapple garden, complete with agrotourism writing in the middle of the garden as a photo spot. Several small huts have been provided for visitors to relax and rest while enjoying the atmosphere of the pineapple garden. According to employee working hours, visitors can visit Prabumulih Pineapple Agrotourism from 07.00 a.m to 16.00 p.m.

In addition to these two tourist attractions, Prabumulih City has many places to visit. However, Prabumulih City Center and Prabumulih Pineapple Agrotourism are popular tourist destinations in Prabumulih City.

Prabumulih City Special Souvenirs


Besides visiting tourist attractions, visitors usually buy souvenirs typical of Prabumulih City. There are several centers for souvenirs in Prabumulih City, one of which is located at Jenderal Sudirman Street, Muara Dua, West Prabumulih District, Prabumulih City, South Sumatra.


Prabumulih City's typical souvenirs are made from pineapple, such as dodol and pineapple lunkhead. Besides that, Prabumulih City has souvenirs made from keluwih and processed into chips, and there is also kemplang and kerupuk.



In addition to pineapple dodol, there is also a souvenir center that provides a typical Prabumulih drink, namely, pineapple syrup. This pineapple syrup is made directly from pineapples grown by pineapple farmers in Prabumulih City, precisely at Prabumulih Pineapple Agrotourism.



The pineapple syrup sold in the souvenir center has several flavors: original, melon, and strawberry. So visitors can enjoy pineapple syrup at their leisure and enjoy it with family.

Conclusion


Prabumulih City has several interesting places to visit for local and foreign tourists. Prabumulih City has tourist attractions and centers for culinary and typical souvenirs from Prabumulih City.


For tourists who want to visit Prabumulih City, they can use private transportation, such as motorbikes or cars, to visit the tourist attractions in Prabumulih City. On the other hand, tourists can buy the typical souvenirs of Prabumulih City to be enjoyed alone or with family.


With this, I hope this will be useful for tourists who want to visit Prabumulih City to travel to tourist attractions and buy typical souvenirs of Prabumulih City.
